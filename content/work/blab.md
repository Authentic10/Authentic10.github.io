<div style="text-align:justify;">Blab is a social network app. All the features are not implemented. The goal was to learn mobile development and know how this kind of app work under the hood.</div><br/>

<div style="text-align:justify;">Users can create accounts using their phone number, follow each other and add new posts. The interactions as comments, likes and messages are not implemented.</div><br/>
